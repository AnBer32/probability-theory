5. $P(158\le X\le 190)=0.9545$

6. $P(X\le150\cup X\ge 190)=0.0241$

7. $P(X\le150\cup X\ge 198)=0.0027$

8. $P(X\le166)=0.1587$

$) $P(X\ge182)=0.1587;$
